Cheese Omelette Recipe

Making an cheese omelette is relatively easy. It is one of my favorite ways to start my day

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • salt
  • pepper
  • butter
  • Your favorite cheese
  • 1/2 tablespoon milk

Directions

  1. Preheat pan on medium
  2. Mix eggs, milk, salt, and pepper
  3. Butter up the pan
  4. Pour the mixture on the pan
  5. Let the eggs cook until thick, take the cheese and put it on one side of the omelette
  6. Flip noncheese part of omelette onto the cheese side, wait until the cheese melts to take plate
