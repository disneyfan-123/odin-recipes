Banana Pancake Recipe

Delicious Banana Pancake Recipe

The following banana pancake recipe is one that I have been using for many months. My family and I love it!

The recipe requires an egg, milk, unsalted butter, all-purpose flour, sugar, salt, baking powder, vanilla extract, and bananas.

Ingredients

  • Whole milk
    • 1 cup or 240ml
  • Eggs
    • 1 LARGE egg
  • Unsalted butter
    • 3 melted tablespoons
  • All-purpose flour
    • 1 cup or 120 grams
  • Granulated sugar
    • 2 table spoons
  • Salt
    • 1/2 teaspoon
  • Baking poweder
    • 2 1/2 teaspoons
  • Vanilla extract
    • 1 teaspoon
  • Bananas
    • 2 ripe peeled Bananas

Steps for success

  1. Get a large bowl
    • The flour, sugar, salt, and baking powder will go in
  2. Get a small bowl
    • The bananas, milk, eggs, butter, and vanilla will go in
      • mash bananas down first
  3. Whisk the contents of the small bowl until everything is mixed together
  4. Take the small bowl and dump the contents into the big bowl, then whisk together
  5. The batter is ready, cook about 1/4 cup of batter at a time
  6. Pancakes will look golden brown when they are ready
